About Us

Colley is a reputed and distinguished educational institution in Maharashtra. Colley was established in year 1934 in Pune by Honorable, Late Shankarrao Kanitkar, along with his dedicated team of teachers known for the highest moral values. It was established by five prominent teachers and experts in the field of education. The motto of Colley is "EVER TO EXCEL". The mission statement of Colley is "Dnyana.
